Contains the Bubble Critic Forums block.

== Description ==
Embed the Bubble Critic forums and build a community on your website.
Main features:
* Free and No Ads
* Cloud-Hosted
--- No Back-End
--- Automatic Updates
* Full Control With Privileges
--- Customizable Privilege Sets
--- Option to Auto-Assign Moderator Privileges
* Issue Tracking Tools
--- Get Forum Users Excited for Upcoming Content
* Share Images, Links, and YouTube Videos
* Smart Algorithms to Promote Best Content

Note: Bubble Critic forums is a third party service. All data that is used to display the embedded Bubble Critic forums is received from bubblecritic.com, and all user input, such as posts and messages, is transmitted to and stored at bubblecritic.com.
Bubble Critic Terms of Service: https://www.bubblecritic.com/tos
Bubble Critic Privacy Policy: https://www.bubblecritic.com/privacy_policy

== Installation ==
1. Upload "bubble-critic" folder to the "/wp-content/plugins/" directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
*Once the plugin is activated, how do I embed the forums into a page?*
When at the page where you want your forums, click on "Add block". Scroll down to the "Embeds" category. Expand the category, find "Bubble Critic Forums", and click on it.

*After the "Bubble Critic Forums" block has been added to my page, how do I setup the forums?*
Go to your live page where the forums are embedded. From there, text and popups will guide you through the process.
